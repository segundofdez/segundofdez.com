Hi, if you have come to this website, I imagine that you will be interested to know something more about me. My name is Segundo Fdez and I'm designer.

In 2006, together with Natalia Crecente and Diego Vilariño, we created Sond3 and, among other recognitions, we were awarded in the II bienal de diseño iberoamericano Bid 2010, in the digital design category.

Since then I continue working on communication projects, combining my profession with active participation in the asociación gallega de diseño, which I belong since its foundation.

Throughout this time, among other things, I have been invited to be a jury in the II premios de comunicación y marketing de Galicia, participate in several talks and workshops talking about design grids or personal projects in the I encuentro de diseñadores y diseñadoras gallegos.
